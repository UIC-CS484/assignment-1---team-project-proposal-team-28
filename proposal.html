Filmie
The project will be based on the TMDB API.
The site will allow users to search for movies and TV shows, find details about them such as a brief description of the plot, cast, trailer and so on.


Users will be able to maintain a list of their favorite shows as well as a watchlist.
Users will be able to review movies as well as leave a comment.


The entities that will model my database schema will be:
USER → id, name, surname, email, password
FAVORITE → userid, movieid
WATCHLIST → userid, movieid
MOVIE → movieid, rating, comments
Anyone interested in movies or TV shows is a potential user of Filmie.
With the growth of streaming services more an more people are becoming passionate about cinema, creating a perfect enviroment for a platform like Filmie.


The main goal of the website is providing movie fans with a platform that allows them to keep track of their favorite shows as well as discover new contents.


I have selected such topic because of my passion for movies and TV shows.
I believe that this tool could be useful for people around the world that share my interest.
